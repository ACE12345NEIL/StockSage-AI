StockSage AI – Technical Documentation
======================================

Overview
--------
StockSage AI is an intelligent stock analysis platform that combines technical analysis, news sentiment analysis, machine learning, risk assessment, and recommendation logic to generate actionable market insights. The system integrates historical market data, real-time news feeds, predictive models, and explainable scoring mechanisms into a unified analysis pipeline.

1. Data Acquisition
-------------------

1.1 Historical Market Data
- Source: Yahoo Finance (yfinance)
- Configurable lookback period (up to 5 years)
- Data fields:
  • Open
  • High
  • Low
  • Close
  • Volume
- Auto-adjusted for stock splits and dividends

1.2 News Collection
Company News:
- Retrieved using yfinance Ticker.news
- Typically provides up to 15 recent articles

Sector News:
- Retrieved through Google News RSS feeds
- Query format: "<industry> industry news"
- Supports regional targeting (India or Global)

Sentiment Weighting:
- Company sentiment: 60%
- Sector sentiment: 40%
- If only one source is available, it is used directly

2. Technical Analysis Engine
----------------------------

2.1 Moving Averages
Simple Moving Averages (SMA)
- SMA20: 20-day average price
- SMA50: 50-day average price

Interpretation:
- Price above SMA indicates bullish strength
- Price below SMA indicates bearish pressure

Exponential Moving Averages (EMA)
- EMA12
- EMA26

EMAs react faster to recent price movements and serve as the foundation for MACD calculations.

2.2 Relative Strength Index (RSI)
Period: 14 days

Interpretation:
- RSI > 70: Overbought
- RSI < 30: Oversold

RSI helps identify potential trend reversals and momentum exhaustion.

2.3 MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
Components:
- MACD Line = EMA12 − EMA26
- Signal Line = 9-period EMA of MACD
- Histogram = MACD − Signal

Interpretation:
- MACD above Signal: Bullish momentum
- MACD below Signal: Bearish momentum
- Histogram expansion indicates strengthening momentum

2.4 Bollinger Bands
Settings:
- 20-day moving average
- 2 standard deviations

Components:
- Upper Band
- Middle Band (SMA20)
- Lower Band

Derived Metrics:
- BB_Width: Volatility measurement
- BB_Position: Relative price position inside bands

Interpretation:
- Upper Band touch: Potential overextension
- Lower Band touch: Potential oversold condition
- Band squeeze: Possible breakout setup

2.5 Volume Analysis
Metrics:
- Volume SMA20
- Volume Ratio

Volume Ratio:
Volume / Average Volume

Interpretation:
- Above 1.0 = higher-than-average participation
- Confirms price movements and breakouts

2.6 Volatility Metrics
Daily Return:
(Current Close / Previous Close) − 1

Annualized Volatility:
20-day standard deviation × √252

2.7 Trend Strength Metrics
- Price vs SMA20
- Price vs SMA50

Positive values indicate bullish positioning relative to trend averages.

2.8 Intraday Range
High-Low Range:
(High − Low) / Close

Measures daily price fluctuation intensity.

3. Sentiment Analysis
---------------------

Framework:
- VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner)

Input Sources:
- News headlines
- News summaries

Output:
- Compound score between -1 and +1

Classification:
- Positive: ≥ 0.05
- Neutral: Between -0.05 and 0.05
- Negative: ≤ -0.05

Aggregated Metrics:
- Average sentiment score
- Positive article ratio
- Negative article ratio
- Neutral article ratio
- News impact score

Combined Sentiment:
0.6 × Company Sentiment + 0.4 × Sector Sentiment

4. Machine Learning Architecture
--------------------------------

4.1 XGBoost Classifier
Purpose:
Predict next-day price direction.

Target:
1 = Price expected to rise
0 = Price expected to fall

Feature Categories:
- Momentum indicators
- Trend indicators
- Volatility indicators
- Volume metrics
- EMA features
- Live sentiment features

Preprocessing:
- Missing value removal
- StandardScaler normalization

Model Configuration:
- 200 estimators
- Max depth: 4
- Learning rate: 0.05
- Subsample: 0.8
- Column sample: 0.8

Training Strategy:
- 85% training data
- 15% validation data
- Time-series split (no shuffling)

Outputs:
- Probability of Increase
- Probability of Decrease
- Predicted Trend
- Confidence Score
- Feature Importance Rankings

Trend Labels:
- Bullish: Probability > 55%
- Bearish: Probability > 55%
- Neutral: Otherwise

4.2 Ridge Regression Forecasting
Purpose:
Multi-horizon price prediction.

Forecast Horizons:
- 1 Week (5 trading days)
- 1 Month (21 trading days)
- 3 Months (63 trading days)
- 6 Months (126 trading days)
- 1 Year (252 trading days)

Target:
Future percentage return relative to current price.

Advantages:
- Stable forecasts
- Reduced overfitting
- Effective with correlated technical indicators

Fallback Logic:
If fewer than 40 valid training samples exist:
- Use recent 10-day return
- Scale proportionally by horizon
- Clamp forecasts within ±20%

Outputs:
- Expected percentage change
- Forecast price
- Trend direction

5. Risk Assessment Engine
-------------------------

Risk Score Range:
0–100

Components:

A. Annualized Volatility
Weight Contribution: 50%

B. Maximum Drawdown
Weight Contribution: 30%

C. Sharpe Ratio Penalty
Weight Contribution: Remaining adjustment

Risk-Free Rate:
- India: 6%
- Global: 4%

Final Risk Score:
Volatility Score +
Drawdown Score +
Sharpe Penalty

Risk Categories:
- Low Risk: < 30
- Medium Risk: 30–59
- High Risk: ≥ 60

6. Recommendation Engine
------------------------

The recommendation engine combines multiple independent signals into a single decision score.

Weight Allocation:
- Machine Learning Signal: 40%
- Sentiment Signal: 25%
- Technical Signal: 25%
- Risk Adjustment: 10%

Technical Signal Factors:
- RSI condition
- MACD crossover
- Price relative to SMA50

Recommendation Thresholds:
- BUY  : Score > 0.15
- HOLD : -0.15 to 0.15
- SELL : Score < -0.15

Confidence Calculation:
Confidence = min(|Score| × 2, 1.0)

Explanations:
The engine generates human-readable reasoning, including:
- Trend strength
- Sentiment influence
- Risk category
- Technical indicator signals
- Machine learning prediction outcome

9. Reliability & Error Handling
-------------------------------

System Behavior:
- Missing news → Neutral sentiment
- Insufficient ML data → Neutral probabilities
- Insufficient forecast data → Fallback prediction logic
- NaN/Infinite values → Sanitized before API response

Production Behavior:
- API failures return structured error responses
- Debug mode disabled in production deployments

10. Performance Characteristics
-------------------------------

Current Design:
- Indicators calculated per request
- No caching layer
- Synchronous sentiment processing
- On-demand model training

Typical Runtime:
- XGBoost training: ~0.2–0.5 seconds for ~1000 records

Frontend:
- Chart.js canvas rendering
- Efficient handling of large datasets
